# Collectables Advert + Market Report — Live-Action AI Video Prompt Pack

For Sora, Veo 3, or Runway Gen-4. Target: 7s, portrait 9:16, 1080x1920. Veo 3 generates native audio (dialogue); for Sora/Runway, generate silent and add the VO lines in your editor.

## Dialogue (student, to camera, ~19 words)
> "Selling rare cards? Just snap them! AI checks real market prices, then writes my advert. Done!"

## Single-shot master prompt (Veo 3 — recommended, handles dialogue)
> Vertical 9:16 video, 7 seconds. A friendly university student, early 20s, hoodie, sits at a desk in a bright bedroom holding a small fan of rare trading cards. He looks into the camera and says: "Selling rare cards? Just snap them! AI checks real market prices, then writes my advert. Done!" While speaking he photographs one card with his smartphone; the phone screen then shows a market-report app with a rising price chart, then a finished classified advert with a price and a green "87% sale probability" badge. He gives a thumbs up at the end. Warm natural light, shallow depth of field, handheld documentary feel, crisp phone-screen inserts.

## 4-shot version (Sora / Runway — stitch in editor)
1. **0–1.5s** — "Medium close-up, vertical: a smiling student in a hoodie fans out three rare trading cards toward camera, bright bedroom, natural light." (VO line 1)
2. **1.5–3s** — "Over-the-shoulder vertical shot: the student photographs a holographic trading card on his desk with his smartphone, subtle camera flash." (VO line 2)
3. **3–5s** — "Macro vertical shot of a smartphone screen showing a clean market-report app: rising green price chart, three recent sold prices, range £24–£32." (VO line 3)
4. **5–7s** — "Vertical shot: phone screen shows a finished marketplace advert — card photo, £29 price, green bar reading 'Sale probability 87%'; student's thumb enters frame with a thumbs up." (VO line 4)

## Consistency notes
- Keep same actor description in every shot: "university student, early 20s, navy hoodie, short dark hair".
- Screens: ask for "clean minimal UI, navy and yellow palette" to match Tuppence branding; plan to replace screens with real app captures in post for accuracy.
- Negative prompt: no brand logos, no Pokémon/trademarked card art, no text gibberish on screens.

## Audio (if generating silent)
Record or TTS the dialogue line, add a camera-shutter SFX at ~2.3s and a soft success ding at ~6s.
