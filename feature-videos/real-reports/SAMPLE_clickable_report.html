🌿 Pretoria Outdoorsy Weekend · Two Friends · Under R1 000 for Two

Currency: South African Rand (ZAR). Emergency: 10111 (Police) · 10177 (Ambulance). All prices are (est.) and subject to change — confirm at the gate/venue. Budget is engineered to stay comfortably under R1 000 for two across the full weekend.

Saturday

Time Plan MarketSquare
08:00 🛒 Hazel Food Market, 378 Queen's Crescent, Lynnwood · Open every Saturday 08h00–14h00. Over 70 stalls offering fresh produce, artisanal foods, and handcrafted goods, with international cuisines including Dutch, Indian, Polish, Greek, and Italian specialties. Arrival tip: arrive early — the most popular items often sell out by mid-morning. Graze on street food for brunch and grab a barista coffee. Est. cost: R80–R130/person (R160–R260 for two). Entry: FREE. —
10:30 🚗 Drive (~15 min) to Pretoria National Botanical Garden, 2 Cussonia Ave, Brummeria · A 76 ha urban oasis situated in the eastern suburbs of South Africa's administrative capital. Scenic walks with beautiful views, a serene waterfall, and opportunities to spot local wildlife like dassies. The trail is "a bit uphill at a few points, many beautiful and unusual plants, very well marked, with benches to sit and rest and enjoy the view of Pretoria from above." Most amazing is the bonsai garden, with lots of art sculptures throughout the park, and a wetland bird hide. Hours: 08h00–18h00 daily. Entrance fee: Adult R80 off-peak (Apr–Aug) / R100 peak (Sep–Mar). For two in off-peak: est. R160; peak: R200. —
13:00 🥗 Lunch at Milkplum Café (inside the Botanical Garden) · A hidden gem inside the Pretoria National Botanical Garden, where you dine under shady trees with a nearby waterfall adding to the relaxing vibe. Open Mon–Tue 7am–7pm, Wed–Sat 7am–10pm, Sun 7am–5pm. Light lunch for two: est. R200–R280. (No extra entry fee — already paid at the gate.) —
15:00 🌿 Union Buildings & Gardens, Government Ave, Pretoria · Towering above Pretoria on Meintjieskop, the Union Buildings are a powerful symbol of South African democracy — stroll through the immaculate terraced gardens and admire the bronze statue of Nelson Mandela. The commanding Nelson Mandela statue — standing 9 metres tall with outstretched arms — overlooks the capital, symbolising peace and reconciliation. Open daily. Entry: FREE. Pack leftover market snacks for a late-afternoon picnic on the lawns. —
17:30 🌅 Golden-hour stroll through Magnolia Dell Park, Muckleneuk · Once a dumping ground, now a lush community park, Magnolia Dell offers shady spots, giant magnolia trees, and babbling streams. Open lawns ideal for lounging, tossing a frisbee, or journaling under a tree; also a top venue for seasonal festivals and outdoor art events. Entry: FREE. —
19:00 🍺 Sundowner drinks at Afro-Boer Café, 1 Meerlust Rd, Equestria · A chilled outdoor setting with plenty of shade from big trees and umbrellas. Winner: Best Outdoor Dining Pretoria 2022, 2023 & 2024; Best Breakfast Spot 2024. Hours Sat: open until late. Two drinks/light snacks: est. R150–R200 for two. —

Sunday

Time Plan MarketSquare
06:00 🦁 Rietvlei Nature Reserve, 14 Game Reserve Ave, Irene · The gate opens at 05h30 — best bird sightings at dawn. Visitors can enjoy game viewing, birdwatching, picnics, fishing, and environmental education. The reserve protects grassland and wetland habitats around Rietvlei Dam and supports a range of wildlife including rhino, buffalo, cheetah, zebra, and various antelope. Self-drive on tarred roads — absolutely, roads within the reserve are generally well-maintained, with most being tarred or easily navigable by standard cars and hatchbacks. Latest time to enter: 16h00 (gates close at 18h00). Entrance fees typically around R68–R75 per adult. Est. entry for two: R136–R150. ⚠️ Fees are set by the City of Tshwane; confirm current tariff at tshwane.gov.za or call 012 358 1811. —
09:30 ☕ Rietvlei Coffee Shop (on-reserve, near the homestead) · The old homestead coffee shop attracts numerous bird species — visitors were lucky enough to spot the brightly coloured crimson-breasted shrike here. Light coffee & rusks: est. R60–R100 for two. (Tip: bring your own snacks to the picnic area for better value.) —
11:00 🦅 Continue self-drive game loop + bird hides · Rietvlei Nature Reserve boasts many bird hides — Island View and Coots Corner overlook the Rietvlei Dam, and a bird hide also overlooks the water at the Marais Dam Picnic Site. Pack a picnic blanket and enjoy lunch at the dam. —
13:30 🏛️ Freedom Park, 1 Koch St, Salvokop · Freedom Park, located on Salvokop Hill, is one of South Africa's most significant national heritage sites, established as part of the country's post-apartheid nation-building and reconciliation efforts. At the heart of the park is the Wall of Names, engraved with over 200,000 names of individuals who lost their lives in struggles from early indigenous wars to the fight against apartheid. Outdoor hilltop trails with sweeping views of Pretoria. Open Tue–Sun (⚠️ closed Mondays — verified). Entry approx. R90/adult (est.) — R180 for two. Confirm at freedompark.co.za. —
15:30 🌳 Wind down at Burgers Park, corner Andries & Vermeulen, CBD · Dating back to 1870, Burgers Park is Pretoria's oldest green space, originally a botanical garden, featuring well-kept lawns, historic sculptures, and paths perfect for a casual stroll — quiet, safe, and close to downtown museums. Entry: FREE. —
17:00 🍽️ Farewell dinner at Green Olive Restaurant, Pretoria · The Green Olive Restaurant is set in a tranquil garden with a calming water fountain; large trees provide great shade, making it ideal for a relaxed outdoor lunch or dinner. Hours: Mon–Sat 7:30am–5pm, Sunday 8am–3pm. Main meal per person: est. R130–R180 · Total for two: R260–R360. —

☔ If the Weather Turns · Indoor Plan-B Swaps

Outdoor Block Indoor Swap — Same Area
Sat · Botanical Garden trails Stay in the garden but head straight to Milkplum Café for a long coffee-and-cake session — fully covered seating area. Still worth the entry.
Sat · Union Buildings / Magnolia Dell Ditsong: National Museum of Natural History, Paul Kruger St, CBD — indoor fossil and wildlife halls. Entry approx. R80/adult. Open Tue–Sun.
Sun · Rietvlei self-drive Self-drive game viewing still works in light rain (stay in the car). For heavy rain, swap to Irene Farm (Irene Village) — two covered indoor eateries and a deli; entry R70. Open 7 days.
Sun · Freedom Park outdoor trails The Hapo Museum building inside Freedom Park is fully indoor — same ticket, same site; powerful exhibits on SA history.
Sun · Burgers Park stroll Church Square — a covered walkable arcade of historic architecture, rain-resistant and central. Free.
