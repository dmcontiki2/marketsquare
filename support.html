The Trust Score is a rating from 70–96 that reflects the reliability and track record of a seller on TrustSquare. It is based on factors such as response rate, completed connections, and account standing. Higher scores indicate sellers with a stronger history of engagement.
If a seller does not confirm your connection request, your credits are not deducted — you only pay when a seller actively confirms. Sellers who consistently fail to respond will see their Trust Score affected. You can browse other listings and send requests freely.

Create a seller account via the TrustSquare admin panel. Complete your profile, accept the seller terms, and create your listing with photos, description, and pricing. Once published, your listing goes live and becomes visible to buyers in your city and category. You must have an active subscription to publish listings.
TrustSquare offers four seller tiers, priced by the number of listing slots you can keep active:

Free — R0 / $0 per month · 2 listing slots
Starter — R90 / $5 per month · 10 listing slots · 2 Tuppence monthly
Pro — R360 / $20 per month · 30 listing slots · 10 Tuppence monthly
Agency — free with verification · 10 base slots that grow with your Trust Score

A subscription buys listing slots, not a time limit — your listings stay live for as long as your plan is active. All tiers include the seller dashboard, photo uploads, and Trust Score tracking.
Listings are not time-limited — they stay live for as long as your subscription is active and you remain within your tier's listing-slot limit. If you cancel or downgrade and end up with more active listings than your new tier allows, the listings above the limit move to draft status automatically; nothing is deleted, and you can republish them from the admin panel once you have a free slot.
Your Trust Score is calculated automatically based on your engagement on the platform. Responding promptly to buyer requests, completing connections, and maintaining an active listing in good standing all contribute positively. Your score is displayed on every listing you publish and is visible to buyers before they engage.
No. You are never obligated to accept a buyer connection request. You can decline at any time. However, consistent non-responses may affect your Trust Score over time. Buyers are only charged when you actively confirm — so declining costs them nothing and maintains a fair marketplace for everyone.
Buyers purchase credit packs to send connection requests to sellers. Credits are only deducted when a seller actively confirms a request. If a seller declines or does not respond, no credits are deducted. All payments are processed securely through Paystack.
Tuppence and subscription payments are non-refundable and cannot be exchanged back into money — they are prepaid consideration for Platform services, as set out in our Terms.

Tuppence and Introductions: Tuppence is only spent when an Introduction is confirmed. When you send a request, the Tuppence is held; if the seller declines, doesn't respond, or the request expires, the hold is released back to your balance in full. A released hold returns un-spent Tuppence to your account — it is not a cash refund.

Seller subscriptions: You can cancel at any time and your plan stays active until the end of the current paid period. We don't issue partial refunds for unused time.

If you believe you were charged in error, email support@trustsquare.co and we'll look into it.
All payments on TrustSquare are processed through Paystack, which supports major South African debit and credit cards (Visa, Mastercard), EFT, and other local payment methods. Your payment details are handled entirely by Paystack and are never stored on TrustSquare servers.
You can cancel your subscription at any time from the TrustSquare admin panel. Your listings will remain active until the end of the current billing period. After that, any listings exceeding the free tier limit will move to draft status automatically. To cancel, log in to the admin panel and navigate to Account → Subscription.
TrustSquare facilitates connections between buyers and sellers but is not a party to the underlying transaction. For platform-related issues (payments, account access, listing problems), contact support@trustsquare.co. For disputes with the other party in a transaction, our team can provide guidance but cannot adjudicate commercial disagreements between independent parties.
