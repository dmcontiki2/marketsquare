How it sits in the app

  • Reuses the real pattern. The filter pill, the bottom sheet, the active-filter tags and the trust badges all exist today (renderFilterBar → openFilterSheet → renderActiveFilterTags, filterState, trustTier()). We extend them, not replace them.
  • Four genuinely new controls: the Trust ≥ slider, the Local · Nearby · Global scope (locked rings open with the subscription tier — a clean upsell, not an ad), the currency selector (prices show in the buyer's money; ZAR-only today so it's invisible at launch), and Save (filter → wishlist alert).
  • Honours the design critique: type floor lifted to ≥12px, icons not emoji-as-iconography in the real build, a visible focus ring on every control, and a guaranteed scrim under any text-on-photo.
  • The results obey the rules: close-first, every card on-filter, tier-labelled, Featured clearly marked and still matching; expansion is always the buyer's explicit, labelled choice.
