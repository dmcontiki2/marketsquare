3 calls that are yours (they shape the build)

1 · The word instead of "Free." Recommend "Team · by application" (or "Custom"). It reads vetted/tailored and stops it colliding with the $0 Free tier.

2 · Billing model. (a) No monthly fee — earn on intros (agents keep 100%, TrustSquare monetises the introduction); or (b) per-seat (e.g. R90/agent/mo incl. their listing cap); or (c) hybrid (small verification/platform fee + earn on intros). This is the "not sole beneficiary" lever.

3 · Agency-level Trust. Keep each agent's own score as primary (recommended) and add a "Verified Agency" badge — vs a single blended agency score. Recommend the former.
