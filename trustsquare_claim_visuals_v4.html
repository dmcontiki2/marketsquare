Claim 1 — Unified Participation Model

One user record; "buyer" or "seller" is a state derived from activity, not a column.

Role is computed, not stored.

Claim 2 — Tuppence Introduction Currency★ anchor

A fixed, buyer-paid unit, burned before the introduction and never refunded — the inverse of a referral fee.

The fee is spent into the dark, and the platform keeps it either way.

Claim 3 — Anonymous Introduction Protocol

No identity crosses until both sides agree — then contact details unlock simultaneously.

Disclosure is simultaneous and only after both consent.

Claim 4 — Trust Score (evidence-based signal-points)

A server-computed 0–100 score from evidence-backed signal-points — earned only on upload or recorded behaviour — offset by a time-decaying complaint penalty. Never self-reported, never user-writable.

Supersedes the earlier fixed-weight composite (30/25/25/10/10) — points are earned by evidence, not assigned by formula.

Claim 5 — Geo-Hierarchical Visibility Gating

Reach widens with subscription, not ad spend — over a four-level geographic hierarchy; travel-planning categories are deliberately borderless.

In production: buyer reach is local (free) or national + global (paid); seller listings extend to additional cities on paid tiers. By design, adventures / experiences, tours, accommodation and heritage listings are borderless — any city, any tier.

Claim 6 — Founding Seller Cohort

The initial per-city founding cohort holds permanent priority placement — a one-time cold-start incentive.

A one-time launch incentive, honoured permanently.

Claim 7 — Combination Claim + AI Services Layer★ lead claim

Claims 1–6 as one system, plus tier-agnostic server-enforced slots, Tuppence-gated AI services, and an AI guardrail.

The combination is the invention — non-obvious even where parts are not.

Claims C10–C13 — Hold-Settled Value Ledger (dual-use)★ charging spine

One hold state machine meters both human introductions and AI runs: commit-on-request → burn on verified delivery, or release on non-delivery — idempotent, atomic, and the only way a balance changes.

Same machine, two service classes; the settlement trigger differs, the ledger invariant does not.

Claim 8 — Heritage Content Linking

Structured place-metadata as a non-transactional overlay on an anonymous listing — the method, not the catalogue size.

The protected idea is the method; the number of sites is incidental.

Claim 9 — AI-Mediated Inbound Communication Triage

Inbound mail is classified by AI, but a reply only auto-sends when a model self-check and a hard category allow-list both pass.

Two independent locks must both open before anything sends — a model false-positive alone can't.
