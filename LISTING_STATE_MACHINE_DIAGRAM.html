The Seven States
DRAFT
Filled in, not yet live. Invisible to buyers. No intro requests. Does not consume a commitment-model slot. Auto-expires after 30 days — nudge at day 20.
Hidden from buyers Seller only Expires 30d
LIVE
Visible in browse and search. Accepting intro requests. The only state buyers interact with. Commitment model auto-pauses on intro received. Free: 2 · Starter: 25 · Premium: 50. Extra capacity: +20 listings per 1T batch.
Visible to buyers Seller-initiated Duration by tier
PAUSED
Was live, temporarily off. Auto (commitment model intro) or manual. Hidden from buyers. Existing intros still active. Fade Out clock continues ticking.
Hidden from buyers Auto or manual
FADE OUT
Stale listing — no intros and no seller login within tier window. Removed from browse. Nudge email sent. 14 days to respond or auto-archived.
Hidden from buyers System-triggered Window by tier
WITHDRAWN
Seller-initiated soft delete. Row kept for audit trail. Reactivatable to DRAFT within 90 days. Auto-archives after 90 days.
Hidden from buyers Seller-initiated → Archived 90d
BLOCKED
Platform-initiated. Verified EULA breach (B1–B6). Listing and account suspended. Cause logged internally. 30-day appeal window. Reinstatement on legal grounds only.
Hidden from buyers EULA-governed
ARCHIVED — Terminal state
Permanent end state. Reached from Draft (30d), Withdrawn (90d), Fade Out (14d), or Blocked (appeal denied / permanent). Row kept forever for Trust Score history, intro audit trail, and version snapshots. Cannot be reactivated.
Hidden from buyers Permanent System-managed

✅ Reinstatement possible — authenticated original + re-verification.

EULA — required clauses (signed before registration)
⚠ Acceptance gate — seller must checkbox-accept this EULA before account creation. Acceptance timestamp stored in users.eula_accepted_at.
§ 1 · Listing Lifecycle
Every listing exists in exactly one of seven states: DRAFT, LIVE, PAUSED, FADE OUT, WITHDRAWN, BLOCKED, or ARCHIVED. The platform may transition listings between states automatically (system-triggered) or manually (admin-initiated) in accordance with the rules in this agreement.
§ 2 · Listing Count Limits
Simultaneous live listing caps by tier: Free = 2 · Starter ($5/mo) = 25 · Premium ($15/mo) = 50. Any seller may purchase additional capacity at 1 Tuppence per batch of 20 extra listings — batches stack and persist until used. Attempting to publish beyond the cap without a purchased batch will be rejected with an in-app upgrade or batch-purchase prompt.
§ 3 · Fade Out (Inactivity)
A listing enters FADE OUT when no intro request has been received and no seller login recorded within the inactivity window for the seller's subscription tier (Free: 30 days · Starter: 60 days · Premium: 120 days). A nudge email is sent 7 days before the window closes. The seller then has 14 days to tap "Keep live" — no action results in automatic ARCHIVAL. Free tier sellers may renew at no charge by logging in within the window.
§ 4 · Suspension and Blocking — causes with recourse (B1–B4)
The platform may only BLOCK a listing and account upon verified evidence of an enumerated cause. All causes are empirical and objectively verifiable. No discretionary or subjective blocking is permitted.
B1
Fraudulent Trust Score evidence
Forged, altered, or misrepresented credential upload. Reinstatement: authenticated original document + re-verification.
B2
Fraudulent listing
Marketing property or services without authority to do so. Reinstatement: proof of authority (mandate, ownership deed) within 30-day appeal window.
B3
Systematic intro ignoring
3+ ignored intro requests in any rolling 30-day window. Reinstatement: 60-day cooling period + written acknowledgement of platform rules.
B4
Buyer harassment
Confirmed contact of a buyer outside the platform after a declined introduction. Reinstatement: first offence only — buyer consent required + 90-day suspension served.
§ 5 · Permanent Block — causes without recourse (B5–B6)
B5
Payment fraud / chargeback abuse
Fraudulent Paystack transactions or deliberate chargeback pattern. Permanent — no reinstatement. Referred to Paystack fraud team.
B6
Identity fraud / deliberate deception
False or stolen identity, or deliberate deception to circumvent the trust model. Permanent — no reinstatement. May be referred to relevant legal authorities.
By accepting this EULA the seller explicitly acknowledges that causes B5 and B6 result in permanent account termination with no right of appeal, and waives any claim to reinstatement on those grounds. Requires separate checkbox at onboarding.
§ 6 · Appeals Process (B1–B4)
The seller has 30 days from the BLOCK notification email to lodge an appeal via the platform's designated appeals channel. The notification states the cause code, evidence basis, and appeals contact. No appeal within 30 days → automatic ARCHIVAL, right of appeal extinguished. Appeals reviewed by a human administrator within 14 days of receipt.
§ 7 · Data Retention
All listing data including ARCHIVED listings is retained indefinitely for: Trust Score integrity (intro counts, tenure, credential history); listing version snapshots (audit trail); legal compliance (blocking and appeal records). Retained data is not visible to buyers and is not used beyond these purposes.
