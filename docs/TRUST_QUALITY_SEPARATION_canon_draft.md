### A·(next number) · Trust and Quality are separate axes (fraud firewall)
- The **Trust Score** measures the SELLER — verified identity, credentials, and
  system-calculated track record. It NEVER includes listing quality, compliance, or
  advert completeness.
- Listing quality is a separate axis: the **Quality Score / Agent Score (SPS)**.
  **Rank = 50% Trust + 50% Quality**, so a verified, low-risk agent who also makes clean,
  compliant adverts ranks higher and wins more introductions — quality is rewarded
  through ranking, not through trust.
- **Rationale.** A skilled fraudster can produce a flawless advert for a non-existent
  product; if polish bought trust points, they could reach a high Trust Score and lure
  buyers with a false sense of safety. Trust must stay non-fungible with presentation.
- Buyer-facing copy must keep stating that the badge certifies *who the seller is*, not
  how good the advert looks.
- **Change control:** Council review required (safety principle).
