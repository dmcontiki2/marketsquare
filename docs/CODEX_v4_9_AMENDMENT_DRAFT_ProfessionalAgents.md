## New section: Professional Agents (Agents-as-a-Service)
1. **Class.** Professional Agents are a distinct participant class in three verticals —
   Real Estate (FFC/PPRA-gated), Car Sales (MIRA-gated), Tour Operators (ASATA/IATA/CIPC-gated).
   One process serves solo agents and agency bulk onboarding.
2. **Reverse introduction.** A private seller/searcher sends a lead at no cost. The agent
   sees the lead (anonymised, with the sender's trust score) and PAYS 1 Tuppence upon accept.
   Decline is free and is never penalised in ranking.
3. **Three scores, always named, always public.** Trust Score (TS): evidence-backed, every
   point maps to a verifiable credential. Agent Score (SPS): average listing quality; for
   agents it expresses technical, regulatory, legal and safety completeness of their adverts.
   Rank = 50% TS + 50% SPS — the only ordering used in agent directories.
4. **Photo anonymity (PHOTO-ANON).** Agent cards never show a recognisable person pre-intro.
   Approved posture: side profile, head cropped at jaw, subject product crisply in focus.

## New section: Benchmark (SUPER) listings
5. One platform-owned benchmark listing per category, pinned first in every sort, marked with
   the red ★ SUPER ADVERT ribbon and the copy 'the benchmark listing for this category'.
   Benchmark copy must never make a numeric claim the same screen can contradict, and all
   photos in a set must show the same item (SUPER-QA rulings).

## Amended rule: filters
6. FILTER-DATA-2. Enumerations that churn with the market or geography (vehicle makes,
   per-city/country lists) are free-text inputs. Stable ontologies remain chips.

## Amended rule: trust evidence
7. Evidence-true scores. Displayed trust scores must equal the sum of the visible evidence
   ledger. The honest ceiling is 90 until referral tracking ships; 100 requires full real
   evidence (precedent: Bee Lady).
