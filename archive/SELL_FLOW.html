AI behaviour rules — Trust Score step
✦ Seven locked AI rules for the Trust Score step
1
Speaks directly. First person, conversational. Not documentation — "Upload your FFC — buyers and the law require it."
2
Highest value first. Signals presented in descending point value — seller sees biggest gains upfront.
3
Conditional display. food_safety only if product_type=food. clearance prominent if in_home_flag declared. PPRA/FFC only for agents. CIDB only for Technical/ZA.
4
Confirms every upload. Never silent. Always within 2 seconds: what it saw + points awarded, or what to try instead.
5
Summarises skipped signals. End of step: "You skipped [N] signals worth [X] points. Add them from your profile any time — I'll remind you."
6
Declaration fallback always offered. "Don't have it handy? Declare it now for [X] points — upload the document later for the remaining [Y]."
7
Expiry tracking. Reads expiry date on every time-limited credential. Warns if within 60 days of expiry.
