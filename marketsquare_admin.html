Local Market — Terms

Before your first Local Market listing goes live

By activating a Local Market listing, you confirm you have read and accept the following terms:

1. Tuppence is non-refundable platform currency. Balances may be frozen if your account is suspended but are never confiscated. Frozen Tuppence is restored automatically when your account is reinstated.
2. Trust Score and visibility. You may publish from day one with no minimum Trust Score. Buyers may filter Local Market listings by seller Trust Score (Established+ ≥40, Trusted+ ≥70, Highly Trusted ≥90). Sellers below 40 are visible to buyers using the default "Any seller" filter. If your Trust Score falls below 30 due to upheld complaints, your listings are automatically suspended until your score recovers.
3. Repeat-offender escalation. First suspension: until score recovers. Second within 90 days: 30-day mandatory cooling-off. Third: permanent ban from Local Market (other categories remain available).
4. Buyer no-show accountability. Buyers who do not follow through on accepted introductions lose 3 Trust Score points per upheld complaint. The buyer's current Trust Score is shown to you on every introduction request — you decide whether to accept.
5. Seller pays on introduction. Your Tuppence balance is debited 1T (or 2T if boosted) when a buyer submits the first introduction request on a listing. This is non-refundable if you decline or ignore. A 1T credit is issued if a buyer no-show complaint is upheld and the listing remains active.
6. EULA updates. These terms may be updated with 14 days in-app notice. Continued use after the notice period constitutes acceptance.
