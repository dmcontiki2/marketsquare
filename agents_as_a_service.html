Agents as a Service — one system, three categories

Every agent in your agency, dealership or tour operation becomes a discoverable, anonymously-ranked professional on MarketSquare. Private sellers and holiday planners choose them from a trusted list — and an accepted lead costs one Tuppence. No bought leads, no portal fees, no cold calling.

Trust Score · the person
0–100

Verified credentials only — the gate certificate, qualifications, track record. Claims never score; ops verifies against the issuing register first.

Listing Quality · the advert
0–100

Photos, complete fields, description, price, area. Coached toward 100 in the wizard; 50 is the publish bar; averaged across the agent's live adverts.

Best Match Rank · the pairing
50% + 50%

Half listing quality, half trust score — area match sorts first. This orders the anonymous list every prospect picks from.

🧳 Trips — the three screens

Plan it with a tour agent? Adventures page · shown to holiday searchers · free intro
  • Better trips for the same money — package rates, route knowledge and operator deals you can't get booking piecemeal.
  • Vetted operators only — no fly-by-night "tour companies" that vanish with your deposit.
  • Your money protected — bonded agents and travel-insurance facilities cover client payments.
  • Itineraries that actually work — connections, seasons, visas and permits planned by someone who does it daily.
  • Help when it goes wrong — a cancelled flight or closed border becomes the agent's problem, not yours.
The risk side is real. Booking directly carries risks you keep: unbonded operators, deposit scams, visa and permit requirements, cancellation terms buried in fine print. A bonded, accredited tour agent carries what they may, and facilitates the rest.
You can always browse and book directly. This is optional.
